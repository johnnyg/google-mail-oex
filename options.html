Google Mail Notifier is a small, simple and fast extension to show the number of unread mails in your Gmail-Inbox in front of a button and the titles of the messages in the button-menu.

Hint: This Extension will only work with your "Google Apps for your Domain"-Account if you have switched to the new infrastructure yet.

GMail verification code (OAuth)

Google Mail Notifier uses OAuth to get access to your GMail-Account, with this autorisation-method this extension will not have access to your password or any other personal information from your Google Account. Get your verficationc code for this extension by clicking the button, grant access and copy/paste the code in the field below.

Here you can manually revoke access for this extension, after that mail-check will not longer work.
