Google Mail Notifier

by Codebit / Tom Schreiber

Google Mail Notifier is a small, simple and fast extension to show the number of unread mails in your Gmail-Inbox inside a button and the titles of the messages in the menu.

Hint: This Extension will only work with your "Google Apps for your Domain"-Account if you have switched to the new infrastructure.
