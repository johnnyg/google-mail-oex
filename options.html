Google Mail Notifier is a small, simple and fast extension to show the number of unread mails in your Gmail-Account(s) in front of a button and the titles of the messages in the button-menu. Furthermore this extension gives you the option to open mail-links (mailto:) directly with GMail.

Your GMail Account(s)

Google Mail Notifier uses OAuth to get access to your GMail-Account, with this authorization-method this extension will not have access to your password or any other personal information from your Google Account. Get your verification code for this extension by clicking the button, grant access and copy/paste the code in the field below.

Here you can manually revoke access for this extension, note that the mail-check will not longer work after that.
